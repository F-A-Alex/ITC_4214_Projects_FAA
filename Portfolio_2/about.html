About Us

Pints & Pages was founded in 2018 by two lifelong friends and passionate readers, Paige Turner and Justin Case. What began as a cozy weekend reading ritual quickly blossomed into a welcoming book club for fellow literature lovers.

Our goal is simple: create a warm, inclusive space where stories come alive and friendships grow page by page. We believe every book opens a door, and every member brings something special to the conversation.

Our Mission

  • Celebrate literature from all genres and cultures
  • Foster thoughtful, engaging discussions
  • Support independent bookstores and authors
  • Enjoy our time off with a well earned beer after a hard day's work

Whether you're a lifelong bookworm or just getting started, we welcome you to grab a cup of tea/ale, pull up a chair, and join the story. We meet every Saturday at The Green Dragon Inn in Hobitton at 19:00!
